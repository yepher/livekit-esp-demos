# 06 – AEC Echo Cancellation

Acoustic Echo Cancellation on the ESP32. Covers why AEC matters, pipeline setup, embedded constraints and tradeoffs, common gotchas, and tuning tips.

## What You'll Learn

- Why echo cancellation is critical for full-duplex voice on embedded devices
- Setting up the AEC pipeline on the ESP32
- Tradeoffs and constraints specific to embedded AEC (memory, latency, CPU)
- Common gotchas and how to tune AEC parameters for your hardware
