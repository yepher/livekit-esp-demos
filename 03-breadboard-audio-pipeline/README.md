# 03 – Breadboard Audio Pipeline

Get to first audio on a plain ESP32 with an external amp and ADC on a breadboard. Aimed at product teams still in the prototyping stage.

## What You'll Learn

- Wiring an external amplifier and ADC to a plain ESP32 dev board
- Configuring the I2S audio pipeline for external components
- Getting bidirectional audio working with minimal hardware
- Practical tips for breadboard prototyping with audio
